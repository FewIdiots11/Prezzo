OUR STORY

Where Pan-Asian meets Italian, honestly.

Little Olives started as a small, limited menu built around dishes the kitchen genuinely loves — steaming udon bowls, folded momos and dumplings, rich Italian pastas, and a handful of mains that don't try to be everything at once.

Guests keep telling us the same thing: warm, cozy atmosphere, attentive service, and a menu small enough that everything on it is done properly.
